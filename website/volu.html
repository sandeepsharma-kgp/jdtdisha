VOLUNTEERS

Children of the Indian origin who have grown up outside India inherit the urge to give something to their motherland . So, many of them want to come to India and help the not so fortunate as themselves in any way possible.They come for a few months and almost allways give to Disha either in cash or kind or both.

Disha has had volunteers from famous universities like Mc Gill in Canada , Harvard, MITand Cornell in USA.They have all made significant and valuable contributions. Investment Bankers, Doctors and Lawyers from USA have also contributed their valuable time and expertise in the areas of healthy living and eating as well as entreprenurial skills.

We welcome volunteers and provide them with comfortable boarding and lodging facilities at a minimal cost.
